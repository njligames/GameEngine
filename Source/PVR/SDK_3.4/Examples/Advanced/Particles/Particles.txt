:Title:
Particles

:ShortDescription:
A demonstration of particle effects with frame buffer blending.

:FullDescription:	
This demo shows a physics based particle effect displaying a total of 900 particles (600 real + 300 reflected). This effect was generated using alpha blending, which involves combining a translucent foreground color with a background colour, producing a new blended color as a result.
