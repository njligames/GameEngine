:Title:
Fractal

:ShortDescription:
A demonstration of drawing a fractal using render to texture.

:FullDescription:	
A recursive fractal effect is demonstrated here using the render to texture technique. This technique is a useful method to create a variety of effects. Basically, a scene is rendered as usual, but in a texture for future reuse.
