:Title:
FiveShpheres

:ShortDescription:
A demonstration of different primitive types.

:FullDescription:	
This demo displays five spheres, each one displaying a different primitive type. These types are GL_POINTS, GL_TRIANGLES, GL_LINE_STRIP, GL_TRIANGLE_FAN, and GL_TRIANGLE_STRIP.

Controls:
	-	Esc	-	Close the application
