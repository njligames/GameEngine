:Title:
ParallaxBumpmap

:ShortDescription:
This training course serves as an example of implementing the parallax bump mapping effect.

:FullDescription:
Parallax bump mapping is a technique for simulating bumps and wrinkles on the surface of an object. This is very similar to normal bump mapping but with the addition of a parallax effect based on the view vector. This training course serves as an example of implementing the parallax bump mapping effect.
