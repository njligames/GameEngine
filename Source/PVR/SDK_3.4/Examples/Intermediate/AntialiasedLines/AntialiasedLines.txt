:Title:
AntialiasedLines

:ShortDescription:
This training course demonstrates how to render anti-aliased lines without enabling full screen anti-aliasing.

:FullDescription:
Full screen antialiasing can be too expensive or unavailable on some platforms, but it is possible to render smooth lines without it. This training course shows how to render anti-aliased lines with round caps using texture filtering and blending.
