:Title:
Trilinear

:ShortDescription:
This demo illustrates the visual difference between different texture filter modes.

:FullDescription:	
This demo illustrates the visual difference between the following different texture filter modes: GL_LINEAR (Bilinear with no MIPMapping), GL_LINEAR_MIPMAP_NEAREST (Bilinear with MIPMapping), GL_LINEAR_MIPMAP_LINEAR (Trilinear).
