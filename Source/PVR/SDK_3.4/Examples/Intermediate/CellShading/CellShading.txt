This training course demonstrates how to implement cell shading.  Cell-shaded animation (also known as Cell shading or Toon shading) is a type of non-photorealistic rendering designed to make computer graphics appear to be hand-drawn. Cell-shading is often used to mimic the style of a comic book or cartoon and is predominantly used in video games.
